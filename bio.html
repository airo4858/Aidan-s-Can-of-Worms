For as long as I can remember, I’ve always wanted to create worlds and ideas. I find that coming up with the concepts for video games and turning those concepts into real games brings fulfillment to that need. I love ideating on the worldbuilding you can place players into, coming up with the game mechanics that players get to experience, and thrusting players into a narrative that they’ll remember long after they finish playing.

My brand of design tends to be pretty goofy and zany, and I thrive into coming up with off the wall ideas and making them work. In my opinion, you could make any game based off of any idea as long as you put in enough thought and effort into it. I believe a creator needs humor and uniqueness to succeed in the modern day, and I apply both into everything I make.

Besides video games, I also have made chess boards, designed mock posters, and devices that tell people to shut up. As I have just graduated from CU Boulder, I will bring my ideas and skills into whatever I produce in the future.
